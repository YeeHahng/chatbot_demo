Frequently Asked Questions – SkyView Suites

Q: Can I check in earlier than 3:00 PM?
A: Early check-in is available from 12:00 PM onwards, subject to availability. There is a RM50 fee for early check-in. Please request at least 24 hours in advance via WhatsApp, and we will do our best to accommodate you. Availability depends on the previous guest's checkout status, so early requests give us time to prepare your unit properly.

Q: Can I stay longer than my checkout time on the last day?
A: Yes, late checkout is available subject to availability. The fee is RM50 per hour. You must request late checkout by 10:00 AM on your checkout day via WhatsApp. If the next guests are not checking in that day, we may waive the fee as a courtesy. Please don't assume late checkout is available—always request first.

Q: What if I have additional guests beyond my booking?
A: All occupants must be registered at the time of check-in. If you wish to add guests beyond your original booking or the unit's maximum occupancy, you must inform management before arrival. Additional guests incur a fee of RM50 per guest per night. Unregistered guests are not permitted to stay overnight. Your security deposit may be forfeited if unregistered guests are found in the unit.

Q: What happens if I lose my access card or door code doesn't work?
A: Immediately WhatsApp management with your unit number and situation. We can issue a replacement access card (RM30 fee) or provide an alternative entry code within 30 minutes. For emergencies outside office hours (8 PM to 8 AM), call the 24-hour security desk in your building—they can let you in and contact management. Please keep your access card safe to avoid repeated replacement fees.

Q: What should I do if something breaks or needs maintenance during my stay?
A: Report any maintenance issues immediately via WhatsApp with a description and photo if possible. Our maintenance team aims to respond within 1-2 hours during business hours (8 AM to 6 PM). For urgent issues like water leaks or electrical problems outside business hours, contact the 24-hour security desk who can dispatch emergency maintenance. Minor issues like loose handles or light bulbs will be addressed promptly. You are not responsible for normal wear and tear.

Q: Can I extend my stay if I'm still here at checkout?
A: Yes, extensions are possible if the unit is not booked for the next guest. Contact management at least 24 hours before your checkout date to request an extension. Rates for extensions are the same as your original booking rate. Extensions are not guaranteed, so early notice is important. If the unit is booked, we cannot extend your stay beyond the confirmed checkout date.

Q: Can I store luggage in the unit after checking out if I have a late flight?
A: Yes, luggage storage is available after checkout for RM30 per bag per day. Your unit will be locked securely, and you can retrieve your bags up to 8 hours after checkout. Inform management of your departure flight time, and we will arrange a convenient storage and retrieval time. Luggage must be collected by 10:00 PM or additional storage fees apply.

Q: Are pets allowed in the units?
A: No, pets are not allowed in any SkyView Suites units under any circumstances. This policy applies to all animals including dogs, cats, birds, and other pets. We understand your attachment to your pets, but we must maintain strict standards for guest health and property cleanliness. If you require a service animal for medical assistance, please contact management in advance to discuss possible exceptions.
