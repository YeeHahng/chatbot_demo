SkyView Suites House Rules

SMOKING AND PROHIBITED ITEMS

Smoking is strictly prohibited throughout all units and common areas, including balconies and staircases. Any violation of this policy will result in a mandatory cleaning fee of RM500 charged to your account. We take this seriously to maintain the quality of our homes for all guests. Please dispose of all cigarette butts responsibly in designated areas outside the building perimeter.

NOISE AND QUIET HOURS

To ensure a peaceful environment for all residents and guests, quiet hours are enforced from 11:00 PM to 8:00 AM daily. Please keep music, television, and conversation at reasonable levels during these times. Parties, gatherings, or loud events of any kind are strictly not permitted at any time, whether during quiet hours or regular hours. Violations may result in immediate eviction without refund and potential additional charges for disturbance.

GUESTS AND REGISTERED OCCUPANTS

Only guests registered at the time of check-in are permitted to stay in the unit. Unregistered overnight visitors are not allowed under any circumstances. If you wish to have friends or family visit, they must depart by 10:00 PM. All additional guests beyond the maximum occupancy stated in your booking must be pre-approved by the management and may incur extra fees of RM50 per additional guest per night.

CHECK-OUT PROCEDURE

Upon checkout, please ensure you leave the unit in clean and tidy condition. Place all keys and access cards on the kitchen counter before you leave. Lock the front door securely and confirm the lock is functioning properly. Send a WhatsApp message to the management team with a photo of the locked door as verification. Failure to follow this procedure may result in a re-keying fee of RM150. Please note that checkout time is strictly 12:00 PM unless late checkout has been arranged and paid for in advance.

FURNITURE AND PROPERTY DAMAGE

Do not move, rearrange, or remove any furniture from the unit without explicit written permission from management. Furniture placement has been optimized for safety and space flow. Any damages to furniture, walls, floors, or fixtures caused by guest negligence will be charged based on repair or replacement costs. This includes damage from spills, marks, dents, or breakage. We conduct an inspection within 24 hours of checkout and document all damage with photographs.

LAUNDRY AND LINEN CARE

Most units include a washing machine and dryer for your convenience. Detergent is provided, but you are welcome to bring your own. Please do not use bleach or harsh chemicals as they may damage our linens. Bed linens and towels should not be washed more than once during your stay unless necessary for hygiene. If you require fresh linens, WhatsApp management and we will arrange a change. Do not dry clean items in the unit or use the dryer for non-standard items. Any damage to appliances due to misuse will be charged at full replacement cost.
